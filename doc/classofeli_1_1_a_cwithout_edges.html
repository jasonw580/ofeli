Detailed Description

The child class ACwithoutEdges implements a function to calculate specifically the speed Fd based on the Chan-Vese model, a region-based energy.

The regularization of our active contour is performed by a gaussian smoothing of phi so we are interested uniquely by the external or data dependant term of this energy.
Fd = λout (I-Cout)² - λin (I-Cin)²
