Detailed Description

The child class ACwithoutEdgesYUV implements a function to calculate specifically the speed Fd based on the Chan-Vese model, a region-based energy.

The regularization of our active contour is performed by a gaussian smoothing of phi so we are interested uniquely by the external or data dependant term of this energy.
Fd = λout [ α(Y-CoutY)² + β(U-CoutU)² + γ(V-CoutV)² ] - λin [ α(Y-CinY)² + β(U-CinU)² + γ(V-CinV)² ]
