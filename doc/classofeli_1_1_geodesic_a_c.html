Detailed Description

The child class GeodesicAC implements a function to calculate specifically speed Fd based on a geodesic model, an edge-based energy functional.

An Otsu's method is performed in order to find the high values of the gradient to converge even when the initialization of the curve is far of the solution.

img_width1Image width, i.e. number of columns. Passed to img_width.
img_height1Image height, i.e. number of rows. Passed to img_height.
hasEllipse1Boolean to choose the shape of the active contour initialization, true for an ellipse or false for a rectangle.
init_width1Width of the shape divided by the image img_width.
init_height1Height of the shape divided by the image img_height.
center_x1X-axis position (or column index) of the center of the shape divided by the image img_width subtracted by 0.5
center_y1Y-axis position (or row index) of the center of the shape divided by the image img_height subtracted by 0.5
To have the center of the shape in the image : -0.5 < center_x1 < 0.5 and -0.5 < center_y1 < 0.5
hasSmoothingCycle1Boolean to have or not the curve smoothing, evolutions in the cycle 2 with an internal speed Fint. Passed to hasSmoothingCycle.
kernel_length1Kernel length of the gaussian filter for the curve smoothing. Passed to kernel_length.
sigma1Standard deviation of the gaussian kernel for the curve smoothing. Passed to sigma.
hasCleaning1Boolean to eliminate or not redundant points in Lin and Lout lists. Passed to hasCleaning.
Na1Number of times the active contour evolves in the cycle 1, external or data dependant evolutions with Fd speed. Passed to Na_max.
Ns1Number of times the active contour evolves in the cycle 2, curve smoothing or internal evolutions with Fint speed. Passed to Ns_max.
